<p><p>The oral disintegrating and dissolving film market consists of orodispersible films and oromucosal films. Orodispersible films are designed to dissolve quickly in saliva, allowing for fast drug absorption without the need for water, making them ideal for patients with difficulty swallowing. Oromucosal films, on the other hand, are placed in the mouth to deliver medication directly into the mucosal tissues, offering localized treatment or systemic effects. Both types enhance patient compliance and provide an alternative to traditional oral dosage forms.</p></p>
