<p><p>The fire-resistant wire market is categorized primarily into single-core and multicore types. Single-core fire-resistant wires consist of one conductive conductor, ideal for direct applications requiring minimal complexity and space. In contrast, multicore wires comprise multiple conductors bundled together, allowing for enhanced flexibility and increased current-carrying capacity, making them suitable for complex wiring systems in commercial and residential settings. Both types are engineered to maintain functionality and safety during high-temperature and fire conditions, ensuring reliability in critical applications.</p></p>
