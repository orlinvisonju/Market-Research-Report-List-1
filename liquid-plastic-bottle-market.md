<p><p>The liquid plastic bottle market is primarily segmented into various types based on the material used. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) offers excellent barrier properties and is widely used for beverages. PP (Polypropylene) is known for its resistance to heat and chemicals, making it ideal for various applications. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) is durable and suitable for thicker bottles, while LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and used for squeeze bottles. "Others" encompasses less common materials, catering to niche applications in the market.</p></p>
