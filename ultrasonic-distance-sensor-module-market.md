<p><p>The Ultrasonic Distance Sensor Module market comprises two main types: analog and digital. Analog types provide continuous voltage output proportional to the distance detected, making them suitable for applications requiring real-time distance tracking. Digital types, on the other hand, produce discrete signals, often in the form of pulse width modulation or serial data, which are ideal for interfacing with microcontrollers and digital systems. Both types see use in robotics, automation, and various measurement applications, each serving distinct user needs.</p></p>
