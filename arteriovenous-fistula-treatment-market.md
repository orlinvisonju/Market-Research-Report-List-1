<p><p>The arteriovenous fistula treatment market encompasses various types, including dural arteriovenous fistulas, peripheral arteriovenous fistulas, and pial or cerebral arteriovenous fistulas. Dural arteriovenous fistulas involve abnormal connections within the dura mater, often requiring endovascular techniques for treatment. Peripheral arteriovenous fistulas occur in limbs, typically managed through surgical intervention. Pial or cerebral arteriovenous fistulas, located in the brain, are treated using minimally invasive methods like embolization. Each type necessitates specialized approaches based on their anatomical and clinical characteristics.</p></p>
