<p><p>The Polyethylene Surface Protective Film market comprises three main types: low-density polyethylene (LDPE), medium-density polyethylene (MDPE), and high-density polyethylene (HDPE). LDPE is flexible and ideal for applications needing conformability, while MDPE offers a balance between strength and flexibility, suitable for more demanding projects. HDPE is known for its robustness and resistance to impact and chemicals, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. Each type serves different needs in surface protection across various industries, ensuring the preservation of surfaces during manufacturing and transportation.</p></p>
