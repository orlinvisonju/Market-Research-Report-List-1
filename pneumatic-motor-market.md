<p><p>The pneumatic motor market consists of three main types: vane air motors, piston air motors, and gear air motors. Vane air motors utilize rotating vanes to produce torque, offering high efficiency and smooth operation, making them suitable for applications requiring variable speed. Piston air motors operate using pistons for a strong output, ideal for heavy-duty tasks. Gear air motors leverage gears for enhanced torque and speed control, often used in industrial applications for precision and reliability. Together, they cater to diverse operational needs.</p></p>
