<p><ul><li>Thermoset</li><li>Thermo-plastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The epoxy resin carbon fiber prepreg market is primarily divided into two types: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset prepregs are characterized by their hard, inflexible structure after curing, making them ideal for high-strength applications in aerospace and automotive industries. In contrast, thermoplastic prepregs offer greater flexibility and can be reshaped when heated, making them suitable for applications requiring impact resistance and recyclability. Both types serve distinct needs, balancing performance, processing techniques, and end-use requirements in advanced composite manufacturing.</p></p>
