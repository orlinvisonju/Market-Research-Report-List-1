<p><p>Industrial robotics in the food and beverage market encompasses various robot types designed for efficiency and precision in food handling, packaging, and processing. Articulated robots feature multi-jointed arms for complex movements, ideal for assembly. Cartesian robots operate on three linear axes, suitable for pick-and-place tasks. SCARA robots excel in assembly tasks with their arm flexibility. Cylindrical robots combine rotary and linear motions for versatile applications. Parallel robots offer high-speed operations with multiple arms for packaging. Other robotic solutions include collaborative robots designed for safe human-robot interaction in food processing settings.</p></p>
