<p><ul><li>Single-Stage Pumps</li><li>Multi-Stage Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal submersible pumps come in two primary types: single-stage and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps consist of one impeller and are suited for applications requiring moderate flow rates and lower head. They are commonly used in residential and light industrial applications. In contrast, multi-stage pumps contain multiple impellers, allowing them to generate higher pressure and flow rates, making them ideal for applications like deep well irrigation or high-rise building water supply. Each type serves specific operational needs effectively.</p></p>
