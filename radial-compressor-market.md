<p><strong>The Radial Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Multi-stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radial compressor market is divided into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage compressors. Single-stage compressors consist of one impeller, providing high flow rates at lower pressure ratios, making them suitable for applications like gas turbines and refrigeration. In contrast, multi-stage compressors employ multiple impellers to achieve higher pressure ratios, ideal for applications requiring significant pressure increases, such as in petrochemical and aerospace industries. Both types serve distinct market needs based on efficiency, performance, and application requirements.</p></p>
