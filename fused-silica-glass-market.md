<p><strong>The Fused Silica Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Purity Natural Quartz Crystals</li><li>High Purity Silica Sand</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fused silica glass market comprises two main types: high purity natural quartz crystals and high purity silica sand. High purity natural quartz crystals are sourced directly from natural deposits and are characterized by minimal impurities, making them ideal for applications in optics and semiconductor manufacturing. On the other hand, high purity silica sand is processed to remove contaminants and is used in glass production, particularly for applications requiring high transparency and thermal resistance. Both types are crucial for various advanced technological applications.</p></p>
