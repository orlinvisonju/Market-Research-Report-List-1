<p><p>Utility Management Systems (UMS) market is categorized into four main types. Software as a Service (SaaS) offers cloud-based solutions accessed via subscription, enabling easy scalability and updates. Platform as a Service (PaaS) provides a framework for developers to build and integrate applications without managing the underlying infrastructure. Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) supplies virtualized computing resources over the internet, allowing utilities to manage servers and storage. On-premise solutions involve installing software locally, providing direct control but requiring significant maintenance and upfront costs.</p></p>
