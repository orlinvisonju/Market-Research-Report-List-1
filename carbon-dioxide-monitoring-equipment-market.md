<p><strong>The Carbon Dioxide Monitoring Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mainstream</li><li>Sidestream</li><li>Microstream</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon dioxide monitoring equipment market is segmented into three main types: mainstream, sidestream, and microstream. Mainstream monitors measure CO2 levels directly from the airway during ventilation, offering accurate real-time readings. Sidestream monitors take samples from a ventilatory circuit and analyze gas concentrations separately, providing flexibility in various clinical situations. Microstream technology represents a more advanced approach, allowing for highly sensitive CO2 detection in smaller sample sizes, making it suitable for critical care environments and improving patient safety.</p></p>
