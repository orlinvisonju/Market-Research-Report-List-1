<p><p>The disposable ileostomy bag market is categorized into two primary types: one-piece bags and two-piece bags. One-piece bags integrate the pouch and adhesive barrier into a single unit, offering ease of use and convenience for users. In contrast, two-piece bags consist of a separate adhesive flange and pouch, allowing for flexibility in pouch changes while maintaining skin protection. Both types cater to different user preferences, with considerations for comfort, ease of application, and lifestyle compatibility influencing market demand.</p></p>
