<p><p>Core drills are essential tools in the construction industry for creating cylindrical holes in various materials. The market is primarily divided into two types: dry core drills and wet core drills. Dry core drills operate without water, making them ideal for applications where water would be impractical, offering greater portability and ease of use. In contrast, wet core drills utilize water to cool the drill bit and reduce dust, ensuring cleaner cuts and prolonging tool life, making them suitable for tougher materials.</p></p>
