<p><p>Magnetic Flux Leakage (MFL) Testing Systems are non-destructive evaluation tools utilized for detecting flaws in ferromagnetic materials, primarily in pipelines, tanks, and structural components. The technology works by magnetizing the test object and measuring the resultant flux leakage, which occurs when there are cracks or other imperfections. This method is highly effective for identifying corrosion, pitting, and weld defects, making it essential in industries such as oil and gas, aerospace, and manufacturing.</p><p>The Magnetic Flux Leakage Testing Systems Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.5% during the forecast period, driven by increasing safety regulations and a growing focus on preventive maintenance.
