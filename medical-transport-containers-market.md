Medical transport boxes are rigid containers designed to protect and secure medical supplies and sensitive equipment during transport, offering insulation and temperature control for pharmaceuticals. In contrast, medical transport bags are flexible, lightweight carriers suitable for transporting smaller items like samples or medications, often featuring insulation layers for temperature retention. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining the integrity and safety of medical products during transit.</p></p>
