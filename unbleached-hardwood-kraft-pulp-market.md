<p><ul><li>Chemical Pulp</li><li>Semi-Chemical Pulp</li><li>Mechanical Pulp</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The unbleached hardwood kraft pulp market encompasses various types of pulp production processes. Chemical pulp is produced using chemical treatments to extract cellulose, resulting in high strength and brightness, ideal for premium paper products. Semi-chemical pulp combines chemical and mechanical processes, offering a balance of quality and cost-effectiveness for products like corrugated containers. Mechanical pulp utilizes physical methods to separate fibers, yielding lower strength and brightness, primarily used in newsprint and cheaper paper grades. Each type serves distinct applications and markets.</p></p>
