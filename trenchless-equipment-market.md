<p><p>The trenchless equipment market includes technologies like Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD) machines and Pipe Jacking machines. HDD machines are utilized for installing underground pipelines and cables with minimal surface disruption, allowing for precise drilling along a predetermined path. In contrast, Pipe Jacking machines are employed for pushing pipe sections through the soil using hydraulic thrust, ideal for constructing sewer, water, and utility lines in urban areas. Both methods promote efficient, less invasive installations compared to traditional excavation techniques.</p></p>
