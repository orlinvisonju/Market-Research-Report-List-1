<p><strong>The Concrete Surface Retarders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-based</li><li>Solvent-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete surface retarders market is categorized into water-based and solvent-based products. Water-based retarders utilize water as the primary solvent, providing easier cleanup and reduced environmental impact. They are ideal for indoor applications and are typically lower in VOCs. Conversely, solvent-based retarders contain organic solvents, offering stronger adhesion and longer-lasting effects, making them suitable for exterior surfaces and harsher conditions. Each type serves distinct application needs, influenced by environmental regulations and performance requirements in construction projects.</p></p>
