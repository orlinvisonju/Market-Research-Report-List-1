<p><p>Barrier tube packaging encompasses various materials that enhance product integrity and shelf life. Plastic options include low-density polyethylene (LDPE) and high-density polyethylene (HDPE), known for flexibility and durability. Polypropylene (PP) offers excellent moisture resistance, while polyamide (PA) ensures gas barrier properties. Ethylene vinyl alcohol (EVOH) provides superior oxygen barrier capabilities. Metal tubes, including aluminum and tin, are widely used for their strength and resistance to contaminants, making them ideal for sensitive products in industries like food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
