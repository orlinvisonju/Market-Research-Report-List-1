<p><p>The carbon steel commercial kitchen knife market is divided into two main types: stamped blade products and forged blade products. Stamped blade knives are created by cutting shapes from steel sheets, offering a lightweight and cost-effective option, ideal for high-volume use. In contrast, forged blade knives are crafted from a single piece of steel, providing superior durability, balance, and edge retention, catering to professional chefs seeking high-performance tools. Both types serve distinct needs within culinary environments.</p></p>
