<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber Recycling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Chemical Process</li><li>Physical Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon fiber recycling market is primarily divided into two types: chemical and physical processes. Chemical recycling involves breaking down carbon fiber composites into their original polymers or other chemicals through high-temperature pyrolysis or solvent-based methods, enabling the recovery of valuable raw materials. In contrast, physical recycling focuses on mechanical methods to shred and reclaim carbon fibers for reuse in new products, offering a more straightforward and cost-effective approach. Both methods aim to reduce waste and promote sustainability in the industry.</p></p>
