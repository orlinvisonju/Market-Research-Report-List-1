<p><p>The oil-filled air compressor market consists of three main types: rotary, reciprocating, and centrifugal. Rotary compressors use a rotating mechanism to compress air, offering continuous and efficient operation, ideal for industrial applications. Reciprocating compressors utilize a piston to compress air in a cyclical motion, known for high pressure output and versatility. Centrifugal compressors leverage high-speed rotating blades to generate compressed air, suitable for large-scale applications requiring a steady flow. Each type serves distinct needs based on efficiency and application requirements.</p></p>
