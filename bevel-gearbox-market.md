<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Bevel Gearbox Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Bevel Gearbox</li><li>Spiral Bevel Gearbox</li><li>Zerol Bevel Gearbox</li><li>Hypoid Bevel Gearbox</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bevel gearbox market includes several types, each serving distinct applications. Straight bevel gearboxes feature conical gears that mesh at a 90-degree angle, offering simplicity and efficiency. Spiral bevel gearboxes have angled teeth that provide smoother operation and higher load capacity. Zerol bevel gearboxes, also called zero-angle bevel, feature teeth designed for quieter operation. Hypoid bevel gearboxes allow for non-intersecting shafts, enabling higher efficiency and reduced noise, making them ideal for applications requiring compact design and high torque transfer.</p></p>
