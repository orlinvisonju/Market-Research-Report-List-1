<p><p>The automotive air cleaner market comprises two main types: dry type and wet type air filters. Dry type air cleaners use paper or synthetic elements to trap dust and particles, offering easy maintenance and replacement. They are lightweight and efficient for most conditions. In contrast, wet type air cleaners utilize an oil-based system to capture contaminants, allowing for longer service intervals and enhanced filtration in harsh environments. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to varying performance and maintenance needs in vehicles.</p></p>
