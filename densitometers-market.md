<p><strong>The Densitometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transmission Densitometer</li><li>Reflection Densitometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The densitometer market is primarily divided into two types: transmission densitometers and reflection densitometers. Transmission densitometers measure the light that passes through a transparent material, such as film, to determine its optical density. They are widely used in photography and printing. Reflection densitometers, on the other hand, assess how much light is reflected off opaque surfaces, making them ideal for assessing color density in prints and coatings. Both types are essential in quality control across various industries, including printing and textiles.</p></p>
