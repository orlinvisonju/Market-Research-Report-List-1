<p><p>Zero Pressure Accumulation Conveyors (ZPAC) are sophisticated material handling systems designed to transport products without creating pressure at the accumulation points. This technology minimizes product damage and enhances overall efficiency by allowing products to accumulate without direct contact. ZPAC systems utilize advanced controls and sensors to maintain a consistent flow, separated by gaps, ensuring that items are not stacked on each other.</p><p>The Zero Pressure Accumulation Conveyor Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for automation in warehousing and distribution centers. The rise in e-commerce and the need for efficient material handling solutions have catalyzed investments in conveyor technologies.
