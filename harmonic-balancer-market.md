<p><p>A harmonic balancer is a crucial component used in internal combustion engines to minimize vibrations produced by the engine’s operation. It typically consists of a weighted pulley that absorbs and dampens the torsional vibrations generated by the engine's crankshaft. This not only enhances engine performance but also prolongs the lifespan of engine components. </p><p>The harmonic balancer market is witnessing significant growth, driven by rising vehicle production and an increasing focus on engine efficiency and longevity. The expansion of the automotive industry, particularly in emerging markets, is fueling demand for harmonic balancers.
