<p><strong>The Micro Dispensing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Contact Micro Dispensing Systems</li><li>Contact Micro Dispensing Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Micro dispensing systems are classified into two main types: non-contact and contact systems. Non-contact micro dispensing systems utilize methods like pneumatic or inkjet technology to deliver precise amounts of fluid without touching the substrate, ideal for sensitive applications. In contrast, contact micro dispensing systems involve direct contact with the material, using techniques like positive displacement or auger systems, which are suitable for higher viscosity fluids. Both types cater to diverse industries, including electronics, pharmaceuticals, and automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
