<p><p>The HVAC contactor market is segmented into various types based on the number of poles. A 1-pole type contacts a single phase, suitable for smaller systems. The 2-pole type manages two-phase connections, often used in residential applications. The 3-pole type is prevalent in three-phase systems, ideal for commercial HVAC setups. Lastly, the 4-pole type is less common but used for advanced applications requiring dual voltage control or additional functionalities. Each type caters to specific needs and efficiency requirements in HVAC systems.</p></p>
